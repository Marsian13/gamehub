⭕ HOW TO PLAY

  • Players take turns placing their mark (X or O) on the 3×3 grid.
  • The first player to align 3 marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins.
  • If all 9 squares are filled with no winner, the game is a draw.
  • VS Computer: AI uses minimax algorithm — play smart!
