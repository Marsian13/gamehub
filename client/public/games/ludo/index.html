🎲 LUDO RULES

  • Roll a 6 to move a token out of home base.
  • Move tokens clockwise around the board based on dice roll.
  • Landing on an opponent sends them back to their base.
  • Safe squares (starred) protect tokens from capture.
  • Get all 4 tokens to the center to win!
  • Rolling a 6 grants an extra turn.
