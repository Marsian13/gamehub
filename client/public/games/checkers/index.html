⚫ CHECKERS RULES

  • Red moves down, Black moves up the board.
  • Pieces move diagonally one square at a time.
  • Capture by jumping over an opponent's piece to an empty square.
  • Multiple jumps in one turn are mandatory if available.
  • Reach the opposite end to become a King (moves both directions).
  • Win by capturing all opponent pieces or blocking all their moves.
