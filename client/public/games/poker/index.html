♠ TEXAS HOLD'EM

  • Each player gets 2 hole cards (private).
  • 5 community cards are revealed: Flop (3), Turn (1), River (1).
  • Best 5-card hand from 7 cards wins the pot.
  • Hand Rankings: Royal Flush > Straight Flush > Four of a Kind > Full House > Flush > Straight > Three of a Kind > Two Pair > Pair > High Card
  • Blinds: Small blind = 10, Big blind = 20.
